Flight Training Tutorial

Introduction

Welcome to our quick Flight Training tutorial! Here, you'll learn how to fly, explore and interact with our exhibits at WildSerpent Safari through TourDrone.

Prerequisites

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection to when flying to stay connected with your TourDrone.

Exploring Controls

To navigate the 3D world:

*** For most users, the best way to experience TourDrone is by exclusively using the keyboard controls mentioned below.
However, for all controls mentioned below, there is an on-screen button that can clicked with the mouse, if you prefer. ***

  1. Use the W, A, S, D keys for moving the drone.
  2. Use the arrow keys (↑ ← ↓ →) to control the direction of the camera on the drone.
    • You may also click & drag your mouse to control the camera, if you prefer.
  3. Zoom in/out with TourDrone camera using the K and L keys.
  4. Press P to capture a screenshot.
  5. Spacebar and Alt keys for ascending and descending.

Interface

Heads Up Display:

  1. At the top left is a Minimap that will track your position live and mark the location of a potential point of interest at your current exhibit.
  2. At the top right is a Timer keeping track of how time you have left.
    • **Note that some days are less busy than others, so be sure to click the Extension button underneath the Timer to see if you are eligible for longer TourDrone access.
  3. Below the Timer is the Main Menu button. Click this button if you wish to end your session early, otherwise continue until your time runs out
  4. The rest of the buttons are the on-screen controls mentioned earlier that can be clicked with your mouse. If you wish to use the keyboard, the controls will be labelled with a corresponding character.

That's It!

Have fun exploring our exhibits at WildSerpent Safari with TourDrone. Now let's start flying!.
