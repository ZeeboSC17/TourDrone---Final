Training Guide for WildSerpent Safari

Introduction

Welcome to the Training Guide to navigating our immersive nature exhibits where you get to experience the thrill of controlling a drone through our breathtaking landscapes. Just read and follow the guide below and you will up and running in no time!

Getting Started

Main Menu Map

To start flying a drone, please click on any of the four locations on the map. Upon clicking, you will be prompted with instructions to either "Fly Tourdrone" or "Join Queue". Please follow the instructions to decide your next course of action.

Gallery

The Gallery page will allow you to see various pictures of our nature exhibits. On the bottom there will be a map just like the one on the Main Menu. By clicking on the clicking on the locations, there will be an overview picture provided on the left of the page. On the right there will be uploaded pictures from previous TourDrone users that you can cycle through.

Booking/Queue

This page will allow you to reserve future TourDrone bookings or directly lineup in a queue for a TourDrone.

Exhibits

Here you will find an overview and pictures of our various nature exhibits.

Flight Training
